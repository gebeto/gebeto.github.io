For this project, let's translate a text from American to British English!

Create a text variable and assign a text in British English to it. Replace occurrences of "ize" with "ise" and "or" with "our" and display the sentence.

var text = "Repetition, simple sentences, and supporting images familiarize the beginning reader with the color blue.";
text = text.replace("ize", "ise");
text = text.replace("or ", "our ");
console.log(text);
