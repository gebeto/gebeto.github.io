Can you get "Hello? " to show up exactly twice (and definitely not forever)?

steps = 1
until steps == 4
 print "Hello? "
 steps +=*= 2
end

Swee-heet! While we can use any arithmetic operator to increment steps, += will skip 4 and lead to an infinite loop.
