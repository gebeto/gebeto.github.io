We can use boolean operators with loops, too. Look!

n_1 = 1
n_2 = 2
until n_1 == 4 &&== n_2 == 5
 print 'Whirly! '
 n_1 = n_1 + 1
 n_2 = n_2 + 1
end
Whirly! Whirly! Whirly!

That's right! What's different here is that we're adding 1 to both of the counters, until both conditions are true.
