An evil villain's disciple found out that "Agent Smith" is onto her boss and wrote a report that "Smith" managed to intercept.

var report = "Boss: the spy's name is Agent Smith. He is 6 ft. tall and has brown hair.";

Change the report: replace "Agent Smith" with "James" and remove everything after the occurrence of ".". Finally, display the report.

report = report.Replace("Agent Smith", "James");
int index = report.IndexOf(".") + 1;
report = report.Remove(index);
Console.WriteLine(report);
Boss: the spy's name is James.

"Agent Smith" will be able to continue the good work, hidden from evil!
