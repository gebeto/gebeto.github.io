As we know, a variable can change its value as a program runs. What if we want a variable to gradually change its value, though?

Let's try!

score = 11
score = score +  112
print(str(score) + " points")
12 points

Fantastic! While everything to the right of the = sign is processed into a value, the score variable to the left receives that value.

Might there be a way that doesn't require us to know the previous value of score?
