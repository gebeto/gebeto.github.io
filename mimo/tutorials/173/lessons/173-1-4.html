Numbers have an important advantage over strings: we can add, subtract, multiply and divide them.

number1 = 3
number2 = 4
print(number1 +-*/ number2)
7-1120.75

See that? We can use the +, -, * and / signs, which we also call operators, to manipulate numbers.

Psst: we can mix floating-point numbers and whole numbers for these operations.
