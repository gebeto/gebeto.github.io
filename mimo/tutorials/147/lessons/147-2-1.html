We need a tag, of course. The hyperlink tag is simple and short: it's &lt;a&gt;.

<!doctype html>
<html>
 <body>
  <a>Take me somewhere!</a><br>
 </body>
</html>
<html>
<body><a>Take me somewhere!</a></body>
</html>
]]>

Thanks! Now, if you try to click the hyperlink, you'll see that it still doesn't lead us anywhere. Why might that be?

Because the hyperlink has plain text in it, it can't be an empty tag, which means it needs a closing tag.
